Mehfil: Where Every Meal is a Celebration

Discover Mehfil, your new destination for an unforgettable dining experience. We blend exquisite flavors with a welcoming ambiance, creating the perfect setting for every occasion. Come, celebrate with us!

Our Special Food Items

Sizzling Bulgogi Delight: Experience the vibrant flavors of Korea with our sizzling Beef Bulgogi. Tender slices of marinated beef, wok-tossed with crisp vegetables and finished with a sprinkle of toasted sesame seeds and fresh spring onions. Served on a hot plate, it arrives at your table with an irresistible sizzle and aroma, perfect with our authentic array of Korean side dishes.

Signature Roasted Tenderloin:Indulge in culinary artistry with our exquisite Roasted Tenderloin. Succulent slices of perfectly roasted meat, cooked to a tender medium-rare, are elegantly presented with a rich, savory reduction. Accompanied by a medley of perfectly tender-crisp seasonal root vegetables and a fresh sprig of herbs, this dish is a testament to refined taste and impeccable presentation.

The Mehfil Platter: Embark on a culinary journey with our signature platter. Savor the crispy perfection of our golden Pakoras, made with fresh vegetables and aromatic spices. Pair them with our freshly baked, fluffy Naan, hot from the tandoor. And to complete the experience, indulge in the rich, comforting flavors of our classic curries, slow-cooked to perfection and served in traditional clay pots. A true celebration of taste!"

Garden Fresh Duo: Begin your Mehfil experience with our delightful appetizer duo. Our crisp Garden Salad features the freshest seasonal greens, bright vegetables, and a light, zesty dressing. Complementing it perfectly are our vibrant Bruschettas, artfully crafted with ripe, juicy tomatoes, fragrant basil, and a hint of garlic on toasted artisanal bread. A refreshing start to any meal.
